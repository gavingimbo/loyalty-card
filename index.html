Member Inquiries

How is validity calculated?

Membership is valid for a rolling 12-month period commencing from activation. Renewal reminders are sent 30 days prior via the Sovereign App.

Can I upgrade tiers mid-year?

Yes. Upgrade to Ruby or Diamond at any time. The difference in the annual access fee is calculated pro-rata.

Are privileges transferable?

Primary membership is non-transferable. However, the Diamond tier includes a complimentary Spouse Card.
