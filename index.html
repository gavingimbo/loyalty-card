Curated Inquiries

How is the membership validity calculated?
Membership is valid for a rolling 12-month period commencing from the date of activation. Renewal reminders are sent 30 days prior to expiration via the Sovereign App.
Can I upgrade my tier mid-year?
Yes. You may request an upgrade to Ruby or Diamond status at any time. The difference in the annual access fee will be calculated on a pro-rata basis for the remainder of your term.
Are the privileges transferable?
The primary membership is non-transferable. However, the Diamond tier includes a complimentary Spouse Card and "Member for a Day" passes for guests.
How do I redeem my Room Credit?
Room credits are automatically loaded onto your Digital Key. Simply present your app at check-out, or instruct your concierge to apply the credit to your reservation invoice.
