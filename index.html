Curated Inquiries

How is validity calculated?

Membership is valid for a rolling 12-month period commencing from activation. Renewal reminders are sent 30 days prior via the app.

Can I upgrade tiers mid-year?

Yes. Upgrade to Ruby or Diamond at any time. The difference in fee is calculated pro-rata.

Are privileges transferable?

Primary membership is non-transferable. Diamond tier includes a Spouse Card and guest passes.
